Nature of project

Part science, part personal study — meteorology meets visual storytelling

I maintain an ongoing collection of meteophotography: photos of dramatic weather phenomena, cloud formations, and atmospheric optics taken with a mid-range mirrorless camera and phone setup. Each photo includes a timestamp, location, and notes on atmospheric conditions. This album is part science project, part personal study, blending an interest in meteorology with visual storytelling. Select pieces are shared in the public domain via Unsplash and Wikimedia Commons.
