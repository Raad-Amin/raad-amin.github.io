F.R. LLC Noteshare Program

Status
Active & growing
Students reached
100+
License
CC BY-NC-SA 4.0
Subjects

Collaboration model
Solo, invite-only, or fully open — depending on course

Named after an old inside joke, the F.R. LLC Noteshare Program is my foremost way of giving back to my school and my peers. It's simple in premise, but large in scope. I structure, organize, and maintain top-quality notes for a variety of intensive Honors and AP courses which I've taken.

Presently, my notes have been used by over 100 students across various grade levels. They've been described as "lifesavers," and many of my classmates credit my notes with markedly higher test scores.

All notes are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International license, meaning anyone can adapt and share them freely.

Student Advocacy Apr 2025

How To Write an Academic Honesty Defense

Origin
36-page personal defense, compiled overnight
Peers supported
6 false-positive cases cleared
Evidence types
Google Docs & Grammarly edit histories, competing AI detector results
Distribution
District-wide; cornerstone of Noteshare Program
Guide covers
Tone, evidence collection, student rights, writing style explanation

After being falsely flagged for AI use, I decided to write a 36-page report detailing how and why my essay was my own product. I utilized a variety of AI detection tools to prove the result found by the district detector was an erroneous false positive.

Subsequently clearing my accusation and supporting 6 of my peers, I created a clear guide to help others navigate the process. It's been publicly shared with every student in the district.
